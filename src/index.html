Who gets advantage from using Gepard
Manufacturers

Every Brand wants its products to look attractive online. But if things go big, it becomes hard to control each reseller. Gepard makes complex things easy, automatically transforming content into the required data model and format.

 Marketplaces

Great product sales demand great product content. But when it comes to data taxonomy, one size doesn't fit all. Gepard overcomes this challenge, aggregating product data from multiple sources and supplying marketplace with corresponding A+ content.

 Online stores

Strong product descriptions are crucial for converting shoppers into buyers. But it's hardly possible to take control of every product if there are thousands of them. Gepard builds end-to-end data flows to deliver content that sells directly to the webshop.
